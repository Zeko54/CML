Profile

Missy

An extraordinary singer, blessed with a voice that effortlessly weaves through octaves, leaving a lasting imprint on the hearts of listeners.

Her vocal range is nothing short of remarkable, spanning from soulful lows that resonate with deep emotion to soaring highs that captivate audiences with their clarity and power.

What sets Missy apart is not just her technical prowess but the sheer versatility she brings to her singing.

Mark

The heartbeat of the band, infusing their music with a magical touch as the resident keyboardist.

His fingers dance across the keys with a skill that goes beyond technical prowess; it's a musical intuition that breathes life into every note.

Mark's keyboard playing isn't just a complement to the band's sound; it's a defining element that adds layers of depth and emotion to their sonic tapestry.

Cendrick

A powerhouse drummer whose rhythmic prowess propels the music to new heights.

His command over the drum kit is nothing short of extraordinary, as he effortlessly navigates complex beats with a precision that anchors the band's sound.

Cendrick's drumming isn't just about keeping time; it's a dynamic force that injects life and energy into every composition. Behind the drum kit, Cedrick is a rhythmic maestro. 

Rommel

Rommel stands as the sonic architect of the band, wielding his guitar with a mastery that transcends conventional boundaries.

His guitar skills are nothing short of awe-inspiring, combining technical proficiency with a deep musical sensibility.

Rommel's ability to seamlessly transition between soul-stirring solos and intricate riffs makes him a versatile and indispensable force within the band.

As a guitarist, Rommel possesses an innate ability to infuse each note with emotion.
