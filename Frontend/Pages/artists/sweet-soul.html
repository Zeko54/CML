BAND

Profile

Arlyn

With a voice that drips with emotion—rich, smooth, and deeply expressive—Arlyn captivates audiences from the very first note.

She carries every lyric with intention, effortlessly shifting from warm, honeyed tones to gritty, impassioned belts that leave a lasting impact.

On stage, she doesn’t just sing—she tells stories, each note soaked in feeling and soul, inviting listeners into every song’s journey.

Borg

Borg is a soulful technician whose fingers glide across the fretboard with natural grace, crafting melodic lines that truly speak.

His playing is rarely flashy but always intentional, marked by bluesy bends, tasteful vibrato, and an effortless command of the guitar.

Whether complementing the vocals or leading with expressive solos, Borg’s guitar adds depth and color to the band’s unique sound.

Arjay

Quietly intense, Arjay’s love for classic Rhodes tones and Hammond organ swells shapes the band’s harmonic landscape.

His hands move fluidly across the keys, layering in warm harmonies, jazzy voicings, and gospel-inspired runs that elevate every performance.

Often lost in the music, Arjay brings a sophisticated, soulful presence to the group, making each song richer and more textured.

Lords

The backbone of the band, Lords anchors each song with basslines that are both solid and inventive, locking in the groove for everyone to follow.

His sense of rhythm and melodic touch bridges the drums and melody seamlessly, giving the music warmth and drive.

Lords’ playing is understated yet vital—always supporting, always moving the band forward with subtle energy.

Obet

Obet’s drumming is the pulse of Sweet Soul Band—dynamic, precise, and always in the pocket.

He navigates complex rhythms with ease, adding clever fills and subtle touches that keep the music fresh and alive.

On and off stage, Obet’s energy and steady presence are the glue that holds the group together, making every performance feel tight and inspired.
